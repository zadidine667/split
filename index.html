1. Introduction

Welcome to SPLIT ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our mobile game.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy applies to all users of SPLIT, including children and families.

Important: By playing SPLIT, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

2. Developer Information

Developer: Nasije

Game Name: SPLIT

Platform: Google Play Store (Android)

Contact Email: naseejedev@gmail.com

3. Data We Collect

SPLIT collects minimal data necessary to provide game features. We collect the following types of information:

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Name: Optional display name for leaderboards (max 22 characters)
  • Username: Optional unique identifier for your profile (max 18 characters, alphanumeric only)

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: A unique anonymous ID generated locally on your device to maintain your game progress and leaderboard position
  • Game Progress: Your high score, coins earned, unlocked skins, and game statistics
  • Gameplay Data: Number of continues used, games played, and performance metrics
  • Platform Information: Device type (Android) for leaderboard display purposes

3.3 Optional Google Play Games Data

If you choose to link your Google Play Games account:

  • Google Play Games ID: Your unique Google Play Games identifier
  • Authentication Token: Temporary token to verify your identity (not stored permanently)
Note: Linking Google Play Games is completely optional. You can play SPLIT and access all features without linking your account.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

4.1 Game Functionality

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Track your high scores and achievements
  • Manage your in-game currency (coins) and unlocked content (skins)
  • Provide offline gameplay capability

4.2 Online Features

  • Display your position on the global leaderboard
  • Show your player name and score to other players on the leaderboard
  • Sync your progress across devices (if using Google Play Games)
  • Prevent cheating and maintain fair play

4.3 Monetization

  • Show optional rewarded video ads (via Google AdMob)
  • Track ad views to grant in-game rewards (coins, continues)
  • Manage in-game purchases (skins) with server verification
No Selling of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Public Leaderboard

  • Your player name, score, and rank are visible to all players on the global leaderboard
  • Your device ID and other information are never displayed publicly

5.2 Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the game:

  • Supabase: Backend database for leaderboard and game data
  • Google AdMob: Optional rewarded video ads
  • Google Play Games: Optional account linking

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

SPLIT uses the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • Purpose: Display optional rewarded video ads

7. Children's Privacy

SPLIT is designed to be family-friendly. We take privacy seriously for all users, including younger players.

7.1 Data Collection

  • Player names and usernames are optional and can be left blank
  • We do not require email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact information
  • The game can be played fully offline without providing any personal information
  • Device identifiers are generated locally and are anonymous

7.2 Parental Controls

  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of their child's information
  • Parents can disable online features by not connecting to the internet
  • Ads are optional (rewarded videos only - no forced ads)
For Parents: If you have questions about your child's data or want to request deletion, please email us at naseejedev@gmail.com

8. Data Retention and Deletion

8.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the game or clear app data
  • Leaderboard Data: Retained indefinitely to maintain leaderboard integrity
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts with no activity for 2 years may be archived or deleted

8.2 How to Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time:

Option 1: Delete Local Data

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps → SPLIT
  3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Uninstall the game

Option 2: Request Server Data Deletion

  1. Email us at naseejedev@gmail.com
  2. Include your player name and device information
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days
Note: Deleting your data will remove your leaderboard position, scores, and all game progress. This action cannot be undone.

8.3 Account Unlinking

If you linked Google Play Games and want to unlink:

  1. Email us at naseejedev@gmail.com
  2. Request to unlink your Google Play Games account
  3. We will remove the link within 7 days

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Server data is stored in secure databases (Supabase)
  • Anonymous IDs: We use device-generated IDs instead of personal identifiers
  • No Passwords: We do not store passwords or sensitive authentication data
  • Limited Access: Access to user data is limited to essential operations only
Note: While we take steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
